The assignment


This Website was created during the 6 weeks of the PRA3006 Programming in the life sciences practical of the academic year 2022-2023. This practical is offered by the Maastricht Science Programme the assignment was to come up with a research question, rooted in the biomedical sciences and to select and use the relevant webservices in order to answer this question.

The research question


The header "Drug Interactions" leads you to the interactive part of this website, where you will be able to explore drug interactions between common drugs used to treat a variety of mental health disorders.
